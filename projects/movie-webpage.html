Movie Webpage Project

In this project, me and my partner made a webpage about a movie that we thought about together.

The hardest part on doing this project was starting it. We came up with many other ideas for this project too, but none of it seem to work. Finally after two days we decided that we would stick to this idea (escape room). I was in charge of writing the story and she was in charge of revising it. After we finished the writing part, the rest was easy because we had our notes.

Some takeaways that I got from doing this project was creativity and callaboration beacuse I didn't actually think that the webpage we made would be interesting, but I showed it to some of my friends and they all said it was nice. I got to know my partner more too by doing this project.

A next plan that I have for myself is time management because we did rush in the end to finish it. *Preview about the project
